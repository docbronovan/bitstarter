If you live in Chicago and own a car you have probably experienced that sinking feeling of walking to your car in the morning and finding a dreaded orange ticket. Ticket Save aims to eliminate that feeling by automatically sending you an email reminder when street sweeping or snowplows are scheduled to be on your street.

Using information from the City of Chicago Data Portal we will match your street with the corresponding sweeping or plowing schedule. If you wish you may also provide some extra information and sign up for reminder emails about city sticker and registration renewal.
